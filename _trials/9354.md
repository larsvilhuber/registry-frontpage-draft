abstract: "The study investigates the effect of choice overload on regret aversion. Participants are assigned to one of the two different size of choice sets (6 options and 30 options), then asked to report their regret aversion preference. To test the hypotheses, we used Qualtrics survey and recruited the participants using Amazon Mechanical Turk. This study is conducted as a Senior Thesis experiment at Skidmore in the Spring semester of 2022. "
