abstract: "Can higher savings be generated using behavioral economics insights regarding procrastination? With five U.S. universities and a large national record keeper, we will randomize mailings sent to university employees encouraging increased retirement savings. Mailings will allow employees to increase savings by completing a simple form. We will randomly assign eligible employees to receive one of several different mailings."
