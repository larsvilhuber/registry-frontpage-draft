abstract: "This study examines the effect of telephone calls on adherence to antiretroviral therapy (ART) medication and ART prescription refills.  Our study population are consenting HIV positive, ART-naive female sex workers (FSW) at 25 participating health clinics in Ethiopia.  We randomly assign consenting study participants to either the telephone call "treatment" arm or the standard of care control arm."
