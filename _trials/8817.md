abstract: "We investigate whether action planning can reduce skipping breakfast. Skipping breakfast increases the risk of future diseases. This is common among young people. Preventing skipping breakfast will reduce the risk of future diseases. In this study, we will examine the action planning helps encourage people to take breakfast using a mobile health application. We evaluate whether app messages that create an action planning about breakfast in the next morning can reduce skipping breakfast."
