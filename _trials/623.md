abstract: "This study aimed to deliver a 5-session intervention which consisted of discussion and activity-based classes on nutrition, food choice, mindfulness, and advertising influence.  The goal of the intervention was to improve nutritional knowledge and skills to empower students to make healthier and more informed decisions about food.  The class was taught in partnership with the Boys & Girls Club of Trenton after school program - Centre Street location.  Physical and survey data were collected to evaluate the effects of the intervention."
