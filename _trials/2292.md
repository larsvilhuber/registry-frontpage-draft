abstract: "In grade 8 and grade 9, all pupils in Danish public schools undergo an evaluation of their readiness to embark on post-compulsory education. One in ready pupils lack the academic, social and/or personal skills to be ready for post-compulsory education. On behalf of the Danish Ministry of Education, the trial tests whether a 2-week intensive learning camp in grade 8 have positive effects on academic outcomes, non-cognitive skills and readiness to start post-compulsory education after grade 9. The intensive learning camps are targeted at pupils who at risk of not being ready for post-compulsory education and are implemented by teachers at schools."
