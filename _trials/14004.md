abstract: "This study aims to understand the impact of goal-setting and monetary incentives on peak-avoidance behavior in electricity consumption. A randomized control trial will be conducted with various treatment groups receiving different interventions, including knowledge dissemination, goal-setting targets, and monetary incentives. The goal is to develop effective and sustainable interventions for shifting peak electricity usage among residential users."
