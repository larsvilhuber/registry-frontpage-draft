abstract: "The project adopts three different treatments aimed at improving students’ learning in rural areas in China. The first treatment provides students "live" instruction online from a highly qualified teacher, the second treatment provides students with pre-recorded, and the third treatment provides teachers and students with teaching resources, teaching plans, short videos and other related teaching materials of urban excellent teachers. This project aims to compare the effects of the three  interventions relative to the control group."
